Execution Systems & Scale
Bringing structure, visibility, and Agile execution to a large organisation.

Agile Transformation & ClickUp Rollout · CedCommerce

CedCommerce operated without a unified Agile execution system across a fast-growing organisation. When COVID pushed teams into remote work, fragmented coordination became a critical execution risk.

Before

Ad-hoc coordination, isolated functional units, high overhead, zero cross-team visibility. Every team operating independently with no shared delivery cadence.

After

Unified workspace hierarchy, standardised delivery cadence, defined task lifecycle workflows. Sprint-style execution across all functional units — 100% workstream visibility.

  • Introduced ClickUp as the centralised execution platform across a 1,000+ employee organisation.
  • Created workspace hierarchy: teams, projects, tasks, owners, priorities, and task lifecycle states.
  • Enabled sprint-style execution across content, engineering, operations, and business teams.
  • Standardised delivery cadence and visibility across 15–20 functional units simultaneously.

How I Work
My operating style is built around clarity, systems thinking, and business impact.

Define

Most bad products are solutions to the wrong problem. I spend more time than most in the problem space — synthesising signals into precise problem statements before a single line is scoped.

Respect

Precision is a form of respect. Vague requirements tax everyone downstream. I write in definitive language with explicit edge cases. Roadmap is never presented as live capability.

Reveal

Edge cases reveal the real structure of a system. What blocks what? That's where the real decisions live. MDC + trade-in coexistence was the spec, not an afterthought.

Align

Visual formats beat long documents for alignment. The right visual removes interpretation from the equation. A capability board does more in 5 minutes than a 20-page doc.

Product Principles

  • Define the problem longer than feels comfortable.
  • Treat precision as respect for engineering and business teams.
  • Use edge cases to reveal the real structure of a system.
  • Design for scale before complexity becomes expensive.

Operating Style

  • Discovery before solutioning — always.
  • AI-native thinking with human judgment where it matters.
  • Clear specs, crisp handoffs, and measurable outcomes.
  • Business growth lens across product, platform, and process.

Problem

Multi-product mobile commerce breaks fast. Plan, MDC, device financing, trade-in, and add-ons carry independent billing logic. Building them as separate products produced billing misalignments, misapplied credits, and manual reconciliation on every bundled order.

What I did

  • Defined 4 enforced dependency rules: Plan ↔ MDC (recalculates on change), MDC + Device anchored to activation date, trade-in credit locked at order commit, plan gates add-ons in real time.
  • Implemented Stripe and iPPay payment integrations — flow specs, error states, retry logic, reconciliation. Engineering spec baseline across 3 sprints.
